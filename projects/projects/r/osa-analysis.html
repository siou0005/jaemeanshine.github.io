OSA Sleep Apnea Analysis

This R project explores predictors of Apnea Index and tests kitchen energy consumption with ANOVA.

Summary

  • Tools: Base R (`lm`, `aov`, `plot`)
  • Models: Multiple Linear Regression & ANOVA
  • Conclusion: Age, neck size, and SBP significantly influence AI
